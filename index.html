"Empowerment through Engineering: Our mission is to design, build, and manufacture a competitive Formula Student vehicle, showcasing innovation and skill. Our Achievements







Pi-EV Competition is platform which provides an opportunity to conceptualize and design a Formula Student Electric Vehicle Powertrain Package which meets rigorous standard set by the Formula Bharat competition. Our journey through the this competition has been a true reflection of collaboration, resilience, and unwavering dedication. The competition's virtual nature did not deter our team's spirit; instead, it ignited our innovative spark, allowing us to explore novel ideas, overcome challenges, and refine our electric vehicle concept.
