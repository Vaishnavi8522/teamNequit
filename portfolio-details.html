Rookie Team of the Year Award

We participated for the first time in FB 2018. In our debut year, we easily cleared the TI Test and the Endurance Test. Due to our excellence, we were awarded the Rookie Team of the Year Award, which is given to a team that shows outstanding work in their first participation.

First Team to Clear TI Award

Team NeQuit 4.0 received the First Team to Clear TI Award in 2018, which is given to the team that completes the Technical Inspection on the first attempt. The award was sponsored by JTEKT.

Cost Report Award

In 2017, we participated in the ‘SUPRA SAEINDIA - 2017’ event and won the 2nd Best Cost Report Award, securing an All India Rank of 20.

PI-EV Award

We participated in PIEV-2023 and achieved:
- International Rank - 2
- Procurement Strategy - 1
- Team Management - 3
- Engineering Design - 4

Formula Imperial 2023

October 2023 marked a thrilling chapter in our racing journey as our team took on the challenge of Formula Imperial, hosted by ISIE India at the iconic Buddha International Circuit. Riding high on innovation, we proudly presented our first electric vehicle “Aarambh,” symbolizing innovation, sustainability, and a promising future. We completed Electrical and Mechanical Technical Inspection, Brake test, and participated in the Endurance Event.
